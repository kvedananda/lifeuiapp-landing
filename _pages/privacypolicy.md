# Privacy Policy

Thank you for using LifeUI. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how LifeUI handles your information.

**The short version:** LifeUI does not collect, transmit, or share any of your data. Everything stays on your device.

<br>

## 1.0 Information We Collect

**LifeUI does not collect any personal information.** We do not gather, store, or transmit any data to external servers or third parties. There are no analytics, no crash reporting, and no usage tracking of any kind.

<br>

## 2.0 Apple Health Data

LifeUI will request access to read/write Apple Health data on your device. This access is used solely to read, write, and display health information within the app.

- Health data accessed through Apple Health **never leaves your device**.
- LifeUI does not transmit, upload, or share your Health data with anyone.
- You can revoke LifeUI's access to Apple Health at any time through the iOS Settings app under **Privacy & Security → Health**.
- If you delete the app, data saved to Apple Health will remain.
 
<br>
 
## 3.0 User-Created Data
 
LifeUI allows you to create your own custom data types and entries. This data is separate from Apple Health and is managed solely by LifeUI within the app.
 
All data you create within the app:
 
- Is stored **locally on your device only**.
- Is never transmitted to any server or third party.
- Remains entirely under your control.
 
You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and protecting your app data from unauthorized access. If you delete the app, all locally stored data will be removed from your device.
 
<br>
 
## 4.0 Advertising and Tracking
 
LifeUI contains **no advertisements** and **no tracking technologies** of any kind. We do not use cookies, pixels, device fingerprinting, or any other tracking mechanisms.
 
<br>
 
## 5.0 Third-Party Services
 
LifeUI does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.
